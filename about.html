About Clean City Portal

Clean City Portal is a digital platform designed to improve waste management in urban communities by enhancing communication, coordination, and accountability between residents, waste collection service providers, and local authorities.

Our Mission

To create cleaner, healthier urban environments by empowering citizens with tools to report waste issues, access reliable information, and actively participate in sustainable waste management practices.

Our Vision

A city where waste is efficiently managed, environmental pollution is minimised, and every resident plays a role in maintaining a clean and sustainable community.

The Problem We Address

Urban areas, particularly in rapidly growing cities like Kampala, face significant challenges in waste management. These include irregular waste collection schedules, illegal dumping, lack of public awareness on proper waste disposal, and limited communication between citizens and authorities.

These challenges result in environmental pollution, increased health risks, and reduced quality of life. Clean City Portal addresses these issues by providing a centralised system for reporting, tracking, and accessing waste management services.

Key Features of the Platform

  • Real-time reporting of waste-related issues
  • Tracking system for submitted reports
  • Access to waste collection schedules
  • Educational resources on waste disposal and recycling
  • Directory of registered waste service providers

Target Users

  • Urban residents seeking efficient waste disposal solutions
  • Waste collection companies providing services
  • Local government authorities responsible for urban sanitation

How the System Works

  1. Residents report waste issues through the platform
  2. The report is recorded and assigned a tracking ID
  3. Authorities or service providers review and respond
  4. Users track progress until the issue is resolved

Impact and Benefits

  • Improved response time to waste-related issues
  • Increased accountability of service providers
  • Enhanced public awareness on waste management
  • Cleaner and healthier urban environments

Project Scope

This platform focuses on urban communities, with an initial emphasis on Kampala. It is designed as a scalable system that can be extended to other cities and regions.
